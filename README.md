# OSRS Grand Exchange Trading Tools

A fast, responsive, ad-free toolkit for tracking and analyzing your **Old School RuneScape Grand Exchange trades**.

This site is built for active flippers who want more than a basic price checker. It gives you a clean way to track flips, monitor positions, review historical activity, configure advanced alerts, and keep an eye on your favorite items in one place.

## What it does

### Flip tracking
Track your trades with support for:

- **Realized and unrealized profit/loss**
- **Historical portfolio viewing**
- **Editable trade history**
- **Backdated trade entries**
- Quick updates to existing flips at any time

Whether you are logging active positions or cleaning up older trades, the site makes it easy to maintain an accurate trading record.

### Advanced alerts
Create highly customizable alerts and receive **SMS** or **email** notifications when your conditions are met.

Supported alert types include things like:

- Interval change
- Price spikes
- Item dumps
- Trade confidence score
- Collective move
- Bucket move
- Item spread
- Threshold-based alerts
- Sustained directional moves

Alerts are built to be flexible, so you can tune them around your own flipping strategy instead of forcing your workflow into a rigid system.

### Item search and market data
Search OSRS items and view:

- **Current traded prices**
- **Historical price charts**
- **Volume data**
- Recent search history for quick access

This makes it easy to go from idea to analysis without jumping between multiple tools.

### Favorites and watchlists
Save items you care about and organize them your way.

- Group favorite items
- Sort and filter favorites
- View multiple items at a glance
- Quickly spot movement across categories

Useful for tracking high-value flips, supplies, niche markets, or anything else you want to monitor continuously.

### Clean account tools
The site also includes:

- A simple **dashboard overview**
- **Signup** flow
- **Account settings** page
- Sortable and filterable views for alerts, flips, and favorites

### Alerts
The alerts system is one of the core features of the platform.

Instead of manually checking charts and item pages all day, you can set up highly configurable market alerts and let the site notify you when something important happens. Alerts can be delivered through **SMS** or **email**, making it easy to stay on top of the market even when you are away from your desk.

The goal is not just to tell you that an item moved. It is to help you detect **tradeable conditions**.

#### What alerts can monitor

The platform supports a wide range of alert types, including:

- **Interval change** alerts
- **Price spike** alerts
- **Item dump** alerts
- **Trade confidence score** alerts
- **Collective move** alerts
- **Bucket move** alerts
- **Item spread** alerts
- **Threshold-based** alerts
- **Sustained directional move** alerts

This gives you the flexibility to track both simple price events and more advanced market behavior.

#### Built for customization

Alerts are designed to be highly configurable so they can match different flipping styles and risk tolerances.

Depending on the alert type, you can configure things like:

- The item or group of items to monitor
- Time interval or lookback window
- Threshold percentages or fixed trigger values
- Volume requirements
- Spread requirements
- Direction of movement
- Cooldowns and evaluation intervals
- Sustained-confirmation requirements
- Notification method

That means you can create anything from a simple “tell me when this item jumps 5%” alert to a more advanced rule set that looks for stronger, higher-conviction trading signals.

#### Monitor one item or many at once

Alerts can be applied narrowly or broadly depending on what you are trying to do.

Use them to:

- Track a **single high-value item**
- Watch a **custom group**
- Scan a broader pool of items for opportunity
- Filter out weak signals and focus on stronger setups

This makes the alerts page useful both for targeted flipping and for broader market scanning.

#### Notification workflow

When alert conditions are met, the platform can send you a notification by:

- **SMS**
- **Email**
- In-app alerting

This helps reduce the need to constantly refresh item pages or watch charts manually.

#### Alert management

The alerts page is not just for creating alerts. It is also built for managing them efficiently.

You can:

- View all active alerts in one place
- Sort and filter alerts
- See which alerts have triggered
- Group alerts for organization
- Edit existing rules at any time
- Enable, disable, or refine alerts as your strategy changes

This is especially useful for traders running multiple alert types at once across different item categories.

#### Why it matters

For active GE traders, timing matters. Good alerts help you react faster, reduce missed opportunities, and spend less time manually checking prices.

Rather than treating alerts like a small extra feature, this site makes them a central part of the trading workflow:

- identify setups
- watch for market movement
- receive notifications
- act quickly
- adjust your rules over time

In practice, the alerts system turns the site from a passive tracker into an active trading assistant.
